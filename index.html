ABOUT 501 CODERS

Key features of our company

Talent

Belize has limitless talent, especially in the Computer Science field, which is why we want to spread the knowledge of Computer Science.

Passion

As the owners of the company, we share a common passion for computer science. We want more people to share our same passion and excel in this field.

Education

As we all know education is our the gem in our lives. To educate youths and other people about Computer Science we will get more people interested into the field.

Oppurtunity

Belize is poised for technology, however, we have not reached our goals yet. With our mission we will be educating more Belizeans about Computer Science which will help Belize to reach it’s goals.

OUR MISSION

501 Coders is an initiative created by two ambitious teenagers in the hope of spreading their love of coding and programming to their fellow Belizean people. Through some crowd funding activities, fundraisers, and food sales, we hope to launch our initiative within the next month.
